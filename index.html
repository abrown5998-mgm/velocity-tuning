What We Do

Tuning Services

From a quick wax before the weekend to a full tune up after a rough season, we have you covered.

Full Tune Up

We sharpen edges and apply hot wax for edge hold and smooth rides. Edges are tuned to factory bevel unless otherwise requested. Base damage can be repaired with PTex for $10 additional.

Detune for the park available upon request.

$55

Edge Tune Up

We inspect your edges and remove burs and small nicks. We utilize high quality files and diamond stones to sharpen your edges. Edges will be set to factory bevel unless otherwise requested.

$35

Hot Wax

We inspect the bottom of your skis or board and remove old gunk that can affect your performance on the mountain. We then apply temp appropriate hot wax for New England (or wherever you shred) to give you a smooth ride. Base damage will be inspected and can be repaired with PTex for $10 additional.

$35

Backcountry Setup

Heading into the backcountry? We offer full splitboard support — skin measurement, cutting, and installation plus binding setup, mounting, and tuning. For skis, we've got your skin setup covered (measurement, cutting, and install), but ski binding setup and mounting is outside our wheelhouse for now.

Pricing varies

Note on severe rust: If your edges have significant rust buildup that requires extra work to restore, there may be an additional $10 charge. We’ll always let you know before touching anything so you can decide how you want to proceed.

About Us

Just a Few Locals Who Love to Shred

Velocity is a few passionate guys that love winter sports, work full time jobs, and tune ski gear in their spare time. We tune our own gear to shred everything from the resorts to epic backcountry. Our goal is to put the same quality into your skis or board that we put into our own. We keep up with the latest industry techniques to maximize the quality out of each tune and do this because we love the process. Our goal is to get your board or skis back to you in time for your trips up north.
